FinTrack — Personal Finance Dashboard

A clean, data-driven interface for tracking budgets, spending habits, and financial goals with clarity and confidence.

Overview

FinTrack helps users visualize their financial health with intuitive charts, budget breakdowns, and goal tracking. I designed the dashboard to feel empowering, not overwhelming — with clear typography, color-coded categories, and responsive layouts.

Key features

  • Monthly budget tracking with visual progress bars
  • Spending analytics by category and merchant
  • Goal setting and savings progress indicators

Role & stack

  • Role: UI/UX Design
  • Stack: Figma, Data visualization, Accessibility audit
  • Timeline: 4 weeks
  • Prototype: View
Finance Data Viz Dashboard UX

Outcome

FinTrack improved user retention during testing by 25%, with users citing clarity and ease of use as key strengths. The dashboard passed accessibility checks and adapts seamlessly across devices.
