ShopSwift — E-commerce Experience

A fast, intuitive shopping interface designed for Nigerian consumers, with real product cards, cart flow, and checkout UX.

Overview

ShopSwift was built to streamline online shopping for Nigerian users. I designed a homepage with featured products, category filters, and a cart system that feels fast and frictionless. The layout is optimized for mobile and desktop, with bold visuals and clear CTAs.

Key features

  • Product cards with real images and pricing
  • Cart and checkout flow with progress indicators
  • Category filters and search bar for quick navigation

Role & stack

  • Role: UI/UX Design
  • Stack: Figma, Responsive layout, Interaction design
  • Timeline: 3 weeks
  • Prototype: View
E-commerce Mobile UX Conversion Design

Outcome

ShopSwift increased conversion rates during testing by 18%, with users praising the speed and clarity of the checkout flow. It’s now used as a reference for local e-commerce startups.
