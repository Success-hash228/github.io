HealthHub — Telemedicine Platform

A clean, accessible healthcare interface for booking appointments, consulting doctors, and managing medical records online.

Overview

HealthHub is a responsive web platform designed to simplify access to healthcare. I led the UI/UX design, focusing on clarity, trust, and ease of use. The homepage features a Nigerian doctor, real service cards, and a booking flow that feels intuitive and secure.

Key features

  • Book appointments with verified doctors
  • Access medical records securely
  • Order prescriptions via online pharmacy

Role & stack

  • Role: UI/UX Design
  • Stack: Figma, Accessibility audit, Responsive design
  • Timeline: 4 weeks
  • Prototype: View
Healthcare Accessibility Web UX

Outcome

The platform passed accessibility checks and received positive feedback from test users for its clarity and ease of navigation. It’s now used as a reference design for local telemedicine startups.
