PosterLab — Lagos Tech Summit 2025

A bold, neon-infused poster design for Nigeria’s premier tech event, blending modern typography with geometric energy.

Overview

PosterLab was created to promote the Lagos Tech Summit 2025. I designed a high-impact visual using neon gradients, geometric shapes, and a silhouette of the Lagos skyline. The goal was to capture the energy of innovation and the spirit of the city.

Key features

  • Bold typography with neon pink and electric blue accents
  • Geometric overlays and circuit-inspired grid background
  • Event details and CTA integrated into the layout

Role & stack

  • Role: Visual Design
  • Stack: Figma, Illustrator, Grid Systems
  • Timeline: 2 weeks
  • Poster: View
Event Design Typography Brand Identity

Outcome

The poster was shared across social media and printed for the venue. It helped drive early registrations and was praised for its bold aesthetic and clarity. It now serves as the visual anchor for the summit’s brand identity.
