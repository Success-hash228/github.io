BrandNova — Identity System

A futuristic brand identity system designed for startups seeking bold, scalable visual presence across digital and print.

Overview

BrandNova was built to help early-stage startups stand out with a cohesive identity system. I designed a logo suite, business card layouts, social media templates, and poster assets — all unified by a bold typographic grid and neon-accented palette.

Key features

  • Logo system with responsive variants
  • Business card and poster layouts
  • Social media templates for brand consistency

Role & stack

  • Role: Brand Design
  • Stack: Illustrator, Figma, Typography Systems
  • Timeline: 3 weeks
  • Assets: View
Branding Identity Visual Systems

Outcome

The system helped BrandNova secure early investor interest and build a recognizable presence across platforms. It’s now used as a reference for scalable identity design in startup incubators.
