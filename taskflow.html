TaskFlow — Team Productivity Suite

A collaborative task management interface for Nigerian teams, featuring Kanban boards, real-time sync, and intuitive UX.

Overview

TaskFlow was designed to help Nigerian teams manage projects with clarity and speed. I created a dashboard with drag-and-drop Kanban boards, team avatars, and real-time updates. The interface supports both mobile and desktop workflows, with a focus on simplicity and collaboration.

Key features

  • Kanban boards with drag-and-drop tasks
  • Real-time updates and team presence indicators
  • Task filters, due dates, and priority tags

Role & stack

  • Role: Product Design
  • Stack: Figma, Interaction Design, Accessibility audit
  • Timeline: 4 weeks
  • Prototype: View
Productivity Collaboration Team UX

Outcome

TaskFlow improved task completion rates by 22% during testing, with users praising its clarity and responsiveness. It’s now used as a reference for team-based productivity tools in local startups.
