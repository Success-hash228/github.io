CityBites — Restaurant Website

A vibrant homepage design for a Nigerian restaurant, featuring real dishes, booking flow, and bold visuals that whet the appetite.

Overview

CityBites was designed to showcase Nigerian cuisine in a modern, appetizing way. I created a homepage layout with a hero image of Suya Platter, a menu section with real prices, and a booking form that feels effortless. The design balances elegance with energy.

Key features

  • Hero section with high-resolution food photography
  • Menu cards for Jollof Rice, Egusi Soup, Suya, and Moi Moi
  • Reservation form with guest selector and time picker

Role & stack

  • Role: UI/UX Design
  • Stack: Figma, Responsive layout, Typography systems
  • Timeline: 2 weeks
  • Prototype: View
Food Booking UX Visual Design

Outcome

The design helped CityBites increase online reservations and improve customer engagement. It’s now used as a reference layout for other Lagos-based restaurants seeking a modern digital presence.
